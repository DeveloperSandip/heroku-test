If you get any error during connection with your atlas DB. Make sure you checked the below steps - 
 
** Check your atlas connection string
** Check your internet connection
** Check your DNS is setted to 8.8.8.8 and secondary to 8.8.4.4 (Global DNS by Google)
** Check whether ip added in atlas as 0.0.0.0/0 (For connection from any ip)
** Check user has access to read write permission
